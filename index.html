HAR Fiddle

Paste your HAR and get a shareable permalink.

  • Your HAR will be stored in an anonymous GitHub Gist.
  • We will not track, view, or store the contents of your HAR files otherwise.
  • Once submitted, your HAR will be loaded in a slightly modified fork of HAR Viewer Example
  • Why did you build this?
    There's no quick way to share, view and analyze HAR that you got from copying from Chrome DevTools. HARViewer existed, but required a URL for your HAR.
  • What is "HAR"?
    HAR is an HTTP Archive data storage format. It is a JSON-formatted data format that logs a Web browsers request information for a given page load.
